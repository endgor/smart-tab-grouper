# Privacy Policy for Smart Tab Grouper

**Last updated:** February 4, 2025

## Overview

Smart Tab Grouper is a browser extension that helps you organize your tabs by grouping them based on their domain. This privacy policy explains how the extension handles your data.

## Data Collection

**Smart Tab Grouper does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

### What the extension accesses:

- **Tab URLs**: The extension reads the URLs of your open tabs solely to determine their domain for grouping purposes. This information is processed locally in your browser and is never stored or transmitted anywhere.

- **Tab Groups**: The extension creates and manages tab groups in your browser. This is done entirely within your browser using Chrome's built-in APIs.

### What the extension stores:

- **User Preferences**: Your settings (such as auto-collapse preference, excluded domains list, and color preferences) are stored locally using Chrome's `storage.sync` API. This data syncs across your Chrome browsers if you're signed into Chrome, but is never accessible to the extension developer or any third party.

## Data Sharing

**We do not share any data with third parties** because we do not collect any data.

## Data Security

Since no data is collected or transmitted, there is no data to secure on our end. All extension functionality happens locally within your browser.

## Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

## Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this document.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the GitHub repository.

## Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. Your browser settings and extension preferences can be cleared by uninstalling the extension or clearing your browser data.
